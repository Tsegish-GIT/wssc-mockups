Customer Service Overview

Welcome to Customer Service

Our customer service department is dedicated to providing you with the support and resources you need to manage your water and sewer services. Whether you need to pay a bill, start or stop service, or report an issue, we're here to help.

This page provides an overview of the services and resources available to our customers.

View your bill, make payments, set up autopay, and access payment history. Multiple payment options are available for your convenience.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I pay my bill?

You can pay your bill online, by phone, by mail, or in person. We accept multiple payment methods including credit/debit cards, electronic check, and automatic bank withdrawal.

When is my bill due?

Bills are typically due within 21 days of the bill date. Your specific due date is printed on your bill statement.

How do I report a service issue?

Service issues can be reported 24/7 by phone or through our online reporting system. Emergency issues receive immediate attention.

What if I can't pay my bill?

We offer payment assistance programs and payment arrangements for customers experiencing financial hardship. Contact customer service to discuss your options.
